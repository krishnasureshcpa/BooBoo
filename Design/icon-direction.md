# BooBoo Icon Direction

## MVP (v1.0)

Use the SF Symbol `ghost.fill` at 48pt for the menu bar icon. This is a system-provided symbol and requires no custom asset. It renders correctly in light and dark mode and at all scale factors.

## Custom Icon (Future)

### Specifications

- Friendly ghost character, simple silhouette
- Color: BooBoo Purple (`#6B3FA0`)
- Format: PDF vector, single scale
- Sizes: 16x16 (menu bar), 32x32 (preferences), 64x64 (notification)
- Transparency: full alpha channel

### Design Constraints

- Minimal: two small oval eyes, no mouth
- Rounded top, wavy bottom (classic ghost shape)
- No outlines or strokes — filled silhouette only
- Must be legible at 16pt in the menu bar

### Assets Needed

- `icon_16.pdf` — menu bar
- `icon_32.pdf` — preferences window
- `icon_64.pdf` — notification center
- `icon_512.pdf` — App Store listing
