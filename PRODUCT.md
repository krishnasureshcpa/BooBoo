# BooBoo

Privacy compliance — reimagined as a living, breathing ghost companion.

## Register

BooBoo is a standalone macOS application distributed via DMG. It runs as a regular `.app` bundle but deliberately breaks from the macOS desktop paradigm:

- **No window chrome** — no title bar, no close/minimize/zoom buttons, no standard window controls. The window is a portal into Ghostly's world.
- **No persistent dock icon** — the app surfaces only when summoned or when Ghostly has something to say. Not a menu-bar agent; a hidden-window app with its own invisible presence.
- **Login-item aware** — optionally launches at login, tucked away until needed.

## User

BooBoo treats *everyone* like a beginner and satisfies *everyone* like a power user.

- **For the layperson / newbie** — Ghostly walks you through everything. The UI is a guided, gamified conversation. Streaks, rewards, reactions, and expressive character animations make compliance feel like tending to a friendly presence, not reading a dashboard. You never face a blank screen or jargon without explanation.
- **For the privacy-conscious user** — deep visibility into what's happening. Ghostly is your partner, not your tutor. Comprehensive status, historical trends, granular controls, and exportable reports — all wrapped in the same immersive UI, not buried in a separate "advanced" mode.
- **For the developer / power user** — full coverage. Every compliance dimension is surfaced, every metric is inspectable, every action is available. The gamified shell doesn't gate access — it *wraps* depth. Developer-grade comprehensiveness under a surface that never intimidates.

One app. Three readers. One ghost.

## Character — Ghostly

Ghostly is the soul of BooBoo. Not a mascot — a partner. An expressive, mischievous, ever-present ghost who reacts to everything you do (and don't do).

- **Name**: Ghostly (user-nicknamed; the character responds to it).
- **Personality**: Playful trickster. Witty, mischievous, celebratory. Teases gently about missed streaks ("Boo! Missed you yesterday..."), throws confetti when you complete a compliance goal, floats around the interface reacting to state changes.
- **Range**: Not one expression — a full repertoire. From sleepy disinterest when everything's fine, to urgent alarm when something's wrong, to delighted surprise when you hit a streak milestone. Like Duolingo's Duo, but mischievous. Like Water Llama's expressive faces, but ghostly.
- **Relationship**: Partner, not pet. Ghostly has opinions, reactions, and a presence. The app should feel *less* empty when Ghostly is there.

## Purpose

BooBoo makes privacy compliance feel like a living relationship instead of a checklist.

- **Guide**: Walks users through onboarding, compliance setup, and ongoing checks — all through Ghostly's dialogue and reactions, not tooltips or modals.
- **Monitor**: Continuously watches compliance status across configured dimensions. Ghostly's mood, position, and environment reflect the current state.
- **Teach**: Progressive education through Ghostly's commentary. No wall of text — learn by doing, guided by a character who explains *why* something matters.
- **Motivate**: Streaks, rewards, levels, and Ghostly's reactions create a loop that keeps users coming back. Compliance becomes a daily habit with a friend, not a chore.
- **Reveal**: Deep comprehensive data for those who want it — but Ghostly pulls back the curtain conversationally rather than dumping raw metrics.

## Experience Principles

1. **Ghostly first.** Every screen, every state, every transition involves Ghostly. Empty states show Ghostly waiting. Loading states show Ghostly thinking. Error states show Ghostly concerned. Success states show Ghostly celebrating.
2. **Hidden until needed.** No window chrome. No always-visible dock icon. BooBoo lives in the periphery and steps forward when it has something to say. Summon it intentionally or let Ghostly call you.
3. **Dark immersive world.** Deep purple-black background, ghost-white text and UI, ectoplasm accent glow. The UI is a space Ghostly inhabits, not a surface with a character pasted on.
4. **Always a reaction.** Every user action gets a Ghostly response — a floating reaction, a position shift, a particle burst. Nothing happens in silence.
5. **Play before work.** Onboarding feels like meeting Ghostly, not configuring software. Streaks and rewards come before metrics and reports. The compliance data is always one tap away, but Ghostly is always front and center.
6. **Depth without layers.** No "simple mode" vs "advanced mode." You get Ghostly's full UI, and everything is reachable through Ghostly's guidance. Newbies follow Ghostly's lead; experts know where to look.

## Visual Direction

- **Color** — Purple-black (`#0A0A12`) primary surface. Ghost white (`#F0EEFF`) for text and primary UI. Ectoplasm green (`#7BED9F`) for accents, glow, and positive states. Ectoplasm blue (`#7BC4ED`) for info states. Ectoplasm red (`#ED7B7B`) for alerts.
- **Lighting** — Self-illuminated UI. Ghostly glows, particles float, elements have inner light rather than drop shadows. The app is a lantern in darkness.
- **Shape** — Rounded fluid forms. No sharp corners. BooBoo is organic, not mechanical. Even data displays are framed in pill-like containers and glowing borders.
- **Motion** — 150–250ms for functional transitions. Ghostly's idle motion is continuous but subtle (a slow float, a glow pulse). Celebratory motion is exuberant (particles, bursts, screen shake on big milestones).
- **Typography** — One family: Inter or SF Pro, weight-driven hierarchy. Single sans face across all UI. 1.15 scale ratio. No display fonts.

## Core Loop

```
Ghostly greets you → Shows current compliance pulse (Ghostly's mood)
→ You complete an action (run a check, fix an issue, explore a metric)
→ Ghostly reacts (celebration / relief / encouragement)
→ Progress rewarded (XP, streak day, Ghostly unlocks a new reaction)
→ Ghostly gives you a hint about what to do next
↺
```

## Ghostly's Emotional Palette

| State | Ghostly Visual | Ghostly Dialogue Tone |
|-------|---------------|----------------------|
| Everything clean | Floating lazily, half-closed eyes, subtle glow | "Boo... everything's quiet. I like quiet." |
| Minor issue found | Sitting up, one eye open, slight tilt | "Hmm. Something wiggly. Should we look?" |
| Warning threshold | Alert posture, both eyes open, glow intensifies | "Okay, this one actually matters. Let's fix it before it grows legs." |
| Critical issue | Full alert, glow flickering, urgent floating | "BOO! RIGHT NOW. I mean it." |
| Streak milestone | Happy bouncing, confetti particles, big grin | "That's NUMBER ONE! You're unstoppable!" |
| Missed streak | Droopy, small, looks away sideways | "You forgot me yesterday... I waited." (playful, not punishing) |
| Onboarding | Curious, approaching slowly, warm glow | "Oh! A new friend! I'm Ghostly. Let me show you around..." |
